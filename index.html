What is Nova Hacks?

NOVA Hacks is a student-led initiative by coding enthusiasts at Villanova University. Collegiate hackathons have been a fast growing movement across the country- these events bring together students from different backgrounds into a common space where they can collaborate and explore their creativity. And we would like put Villanova on the map as a hub for creative, collaborative, novel and innovative programming. Our vision in organizing the first NOVA Hacks is to bring in a hundred plus hacking enthusiasts with varied experiences for a two-day event where they will work to bring their ideas to life.
